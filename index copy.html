ABOUT MAUD THE GUIDE BEHIND THE LENS
9/11 MEMORIAL · NEW YORK · 2024

Curiosity is my compass.
The world is my classroom.

I've stood at the edge of Patagonian glaciers, walked through the winding bazaars of Istanbul, felt the electricity of Times Square, and breathed the salt air of the Mediterranean coast. Every journey has taught me something irreplaceable.

As a tourist guide, my goal is simple: to share not just places, but moments. The kind that stay with you long after you've unpacked your bags. I speak the language of discovery, because I've spent years learning it firsthand.

Whether it's finding the hidden café in a Buenos Aires neighborhood, timing the golden light on Torres del Paine, or knowing exactly when the crowds leave the Grand Bazaar, the details make all the difference.
