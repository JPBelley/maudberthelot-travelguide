ABOUT MAUD GEOGRAPHER · GUIDE · EXPLORER
9/11 MEMORIAL · NEW YORK · 2024

Geographer.
Guide.
Lover of territories.

I am a geographer, guide, and great lover of territories — those we study, but above all those we feel.

What drives me most is the human experience of travel: telling stories, accompanying people, creating an atmosphere, and ensuring that a place leaves a lasting impression.

THE BEGINNING BRITISH COLUMBIA · 18 YEARS OLD

Standing before the mountains of Western Canada, I understood that the world was infinite.

My desire to travel began at 18, when I left to spend a year in British Columbia to learn English. Facing the mountains of the Canadian West, I understood that there was an infinity of landscapes, cultures, and ways of inhabiting the world waiting to be discovered.

Since then, travels have shaped my life as much as my professional journey.

THE TERRITORIES WORLD · 2018 – PRESENT
TORRES DEL PAINE · PATAGONIA

I have explored the vast open spaces of the American West, Chilean and Argentine Patagonia, the Andalusian cities, Italy, Amsterdam, Singapore, Mexico, and many other places that have marked me as much for their landscapes as for their cultural and human identity.

I am particularly fascinated by places where nature, urbanism, history, and art de vivre meet — like Barcelona or Singapore, whose balance between development and conservation continues to inspire me.

SOLO AROUND THE WORLD LONDON → MALAYSIA · TWO MONTHS
CROATIA · ISTANBUL · SYDNEY

Traveling alone taught me to observe, to slow down, and to find the details that make an experience unforgettable.

For two months, I traveled solo around the world — from London to Malaysia, by way of Croatia, Montenegro, Istanbul, and Australia.

This kind of journey sharpened my sensitivity to the details that transform a visit into a memory: the quality of light on a particular street, the rhythm of a neighborhood at dusk, the precise moment a place reveals itself.

WHAT DRIVES ME
ISTANBUL · TURKEY · 2024
